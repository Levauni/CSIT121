Jumbo Lump Crab Cakes

Baltimore Style


Ingredients

  • 1 pound jumbo lump crab meat
  • 1/2 cup of bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of old bay seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons of parsely
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon of mustard

This is my favorite recipe because its easy, and very delicious. I've lived in a couple different places and no place in the world makes crab cakes like Baltimore and a of couple of the surrouning areas.
