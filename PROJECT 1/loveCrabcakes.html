Why I Love Crabcakes


FYI: Crabcakes are one of my childhood favorites that has becomee a holy grail item in my kitchen! And just to be clear, I ONLY eat crab cakes at resturants in Maryland or cook them myself at home.

  • Cabcakes are very simply to make, with less than ten ingredients!
  • Crabcakes are my comfort food, and reminds me of home. When I lived in California I would make them all the time.
  • Crabcakes are delicious, plain and simple
